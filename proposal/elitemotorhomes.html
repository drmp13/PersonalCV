Introduction

I'm excited to present this proposal for the development of a new motorhome sales website (https://elitemotorhomes.co.uk). As the digital landscape evolves, it's crucial to ensure that your online presence remains fresh, engaging, and user-friendly. With this in mind, we propose to revamp your current website, providing an updated platform that effectively showcases your motorhome offerings and enhances user experience. With extensive experience in web development and a commitment to delivering high-quality solutions, I am confident in my ability to create new motorhomes sales website tailored to your requirements.

Objective Goals

The primary objectives of this project include:
  • Designing and developing a user-friendly website with intuitive navigation.
  • Showcasing motorhome products effectively, allowing users to filter and compare options.
  • Integrating essential features such as a finance calculator, contact forms, and email subscription for news/promotional.
  • Implementing a content management system (CMS) for easy website management.
  • Ensuring seamless integration with Autotrader for automatic vehicle listings.
  • Incorporating SEO best practices to enhance online visibility.

Core Scope of Work

The proposed website will include the following key features and functionalities:
  1. About Us Section:
    • Provide information about your company, its history, and mission.
  2. Benefits of Buying Section:
    • Highlight the advantages of purchasing motorhomes from your dealership.
  3. Testimonial Section:
    • Showcase customer testimonials to build trust and credibility.
  4. News/Blog Section:
    • Implement a blog/news section to share industry insights, company updates, and relevant content.
  5. Finance Calculator:
    • Develop a finance calculator tool to help customers estimate financing options for motorhome purchases.
  6. Contact Page:
    • Contact Form:
      • Include a contact form for inquiries and feedback.
    • Opening Times:
      • Display your dealership's operating hours for customer convenience.
  7. Shortlink to Elite Specialist Repair:
    • Integrate a link to Elite Specialist Repair services, potentially under the Service section for easy access.
  8. Vehicle Listings:
    • Product List:
      • Create a comprehensive list of motorhomes with filtering options based on price range, body style, manufacturer, year, berth, transmission, fuel type, exterior color, and condition. Emphasize weight and length of vehicles prominently.
    • Product Comparison:
      • Enable users to compare multiple motorhomes side by side for informed decision-making.
    • Product Detail & Gallery:
      • Provide detailed descriptions and high-quality image galleries for each motorhome listing.
  9. Accessories:
    • List accessories available for purchase, enhancing the customer's motorhome experience.
  10. Aftersales:
    • Aftersales Form:
      • Implement a form for customers to submit inquiries or requests for aftersales services.
  11. Servicing Information:
    • Develop an informational page detailing servicing options and procedures.
  12. Meet the Team:
    • Create a dedicated page introducing your team members, fostering a personal connection with potential customers.
  13. Email Capture:
    • Implement an email subscription feature to capture visitor information for newsletters, product updates, and promotions.
  14. CMS Integration:
    • Build the website on a user-friendly Content Management System (CMS) to facilitate easy management of content, vehicle listings, SEO optimization, web banners, and advertisements. Ensure seamless integration with Autotrader for automatic listing updates.

Development Approach

  • I will utilize the provided mockup designs for as a reference for the website's visual appearance and layout.
  • The website will be developed using modern web development technologies, ensuring compatibility across various devices and browsers.
  • Integration with a user-friendly CMS will enable easy content management, including vehicle additions, SEO management, and banner/advertisement updates.
  • I have provided mockup designs for main website (that accessed by client).
  • I do not provide a mockup design for the administrator CMS dashboard because this dashboard will be developed to suit the content presented on the client's website. It is certain that the website accessed by the client will be fully customized with the CMS available in the admin dashboard. The needs of the Elite Motorhomes team regarding this admin dashboard can always be discussed during the development process.
